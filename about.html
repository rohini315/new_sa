about

Save the Starving Artists believes in fostering creativity among young, up and coming artists. SSA incorporates a unique spatial, modern and flexible studio design, with windowed studios that provides a loft-type feel. The studio spaces allow artists to grow and also collaborate with artists working in different mediums. SSA also has strong relations with various NYC galleries and auction houses, and they provide artists with buisness and marketing advice. SSA expands the boundary of the art community into various fields.

facilities

SSA facilities allow each artist to have his or her own studio space equipped with everything needed to create and collaborate. SSA facilities include:

  • Fully stocked kitchen, with an espresso machine
  • Various roomy lounge areas
  • State of the art technologies
  • Discounts to art supplies stores
  • Dark Room and Digital Print Lab
  • Basic woodshop/metalshop/ceramic studio
  • Design Library
